CURIOUS FACT

The addiction of children to video games

Videogames, in small doses, are not bad: they improve the child's deductive capacity, they exercise their reflexes, it is good for them to learn more easily the spatial conception, to overcome challenges, to decide quickly ... But when video games they become an obsession, problems come: the child blurs the boundary between reality and fiction, becomes less sociable, does not perform enough physical activity, becomes more irascible and anxious ...
